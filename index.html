Types of Electric Vehicles

Discover the full spectrum of electric vehicle technologies

Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs)

100% electric vehicles powered solely by rechargeable batteries with no gasoline engine.

Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs)

Vehicles with both electric motors and gas engines that can be plugged in to recharge.

Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs)

Vehicles that combine a conventional engine with an electric motor to improve efficiency.

Fuel Cell Electric Vehicles (FCEVs)

Vehicles powered by hydrogen fuel cells that generate electricity, emitting only water vapor.
